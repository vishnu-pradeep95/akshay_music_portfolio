About

Akshay Sreeram is a versatile music director hailing from the culturally rich state of Kerala, India. Unlike many in his field who follow traditional paths to success through formal education, Akshay is largely self-taught, a testament to his innate talent and dedication to his craft.

His unique background in both mechanical and audio engineering lends a multidimensional depth to his work, allowing him to approach compositions with technical precision as well as artistic flair. Akshay has been a significant contributor to numerous international titles, where his compositions have been lauded for their originality and emotional resonance.

A synthesis of traditional roots and modern influences, Akshay's music serves as a bridge between the old and the new, resonating with a global audience while staying true to his origins. His journey so far is a compelling narrative of passion, self-belief, and ceaseless innovation.
